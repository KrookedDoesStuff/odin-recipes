Krooked's Honey Mustard

This is Krooked's honey mustard recipe. He was struggling to find a perfect condiment for chickie nuggies and stumbled across this through some wild experimentation

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Mustard
  • 1 cup Local Honey
  • 1 tbs Cayenne Pepper

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients together
  2. Whisk until smooth
  3. Taste for seasoning
