Noni's Carbonara Recipe

This is my Nonni's fabulous Carbonara

This is a Carbonara dish that was created by my grandmother and updated with modern ingredients by me

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Spaghetti
  • 4 slices of cooked bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • Diced ham
  • Sliced black olives
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon
  2. Add butter and ham to pan and cook til browned
  3. Add garlic and cook for around 30 seconds
  4. Cook spaghetti and drain
  5. Add all ingredients except egg
  6. Add egg and stir vigorously
  7. Serve immediately
